Today we are introducing a new product name for our always-on personal AI system:

## OpenClaw Autopilot

OpenClaw Autopilot is built for people who want more than a chatbot.

Most assistants reset every session. OpenClaw Autopilot does not.

It lives in messaging apps like WhatsApp and Telegram, keeps long-term context, and continues working in the background. Over time, it learns your preferences, adopts your workflows, and becomes a practical digital autopilot you can rely on every day.

## What makes it different

- **Persistent memory:** it remembers your context across conversations.
- **Chat-native interface:** use it from the apps you already open all day.
- **Tool-connected:** connect email, calendar, and more.
- **Multi-model backend:** run on leading AI models behind the scenes.
- **24/7 operation:** monitor, notify, and act even while you sleep.
